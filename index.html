Online Benefits

With our online journey numbers of benefits are available for our members. Salient features are described below:

  • Personalised e-mail : Our customized e-mail id like yourname@oraa-85.ml for every members alongwith thier spouse and childrens
  • Personal Disk Space : 10 GB online disk space attached to the mailbox for personal use that can store personal land documents, image or video and can be sharable with anybody in the world.
  • Translator : A built-in-translator helps to read or send mails in own preferred languages.
  • IMAP & POP : Mailboxes are accessible through IMAP and POP. It can be set up conveniently in desktop and mobile email program. Easily managable with Gmail, Outlook and Yahoo even with Thunderbird.
  • Task manager : A simple task manager built into mailbox lets anybody to kepp a to-do list.
  • Virus Filter : A spam and virus filter (Dr.Web) moves out virus-laden, junk, phishing mails.
