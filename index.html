About Rajshahi University

About us

University of Rajshahi is one of the largest Universities in the country and the largest with the highest seat of learning in the northern region of Bangladesh. After its foundation on July 6, 1953, the university has been providing higher education and research. The university is located at the green premises of Motihar which is very close to the mighty river Padma and seven km. east from the Rajshahi City Center.

Since the inception of the Accounting Department, University of Rajshahi (presently it is reorganized as Accounting and Information System Department under the Faculty of Business Studies), it has produced a significant number of graduates, many of whom are well experienced and established. In this scenario, we are the ex-students of the Accounting Department, batch-1985, feels to organize ourselves for achieving our common socio-economic goal. While many of our classmates are working and living in Dhaka city, numbers of initiative are taken to establish a platform here. As a result in September, 2005 the Old Rajshahi University Accounting Association-1985 in short ORAA-85 is established.

Presently there are two types of membership in ORAA-85 e.g. Full Membership includes classmates of the Accounting Department, batch-1985 and remaining Associate Membership includes full member's spouse, children plus friendly interested group of people whom are invested in ORAA-85 commercial project.

ORAA-85 has been organizing numbers of member's meeting, family event and annual picnic on regular basis. Family members of ORAA-85 are being congratulated and prized for obatining GPA-5 result in JSC, SSC, HSC exam.

Total 34.60 decimal of land located at Ranavola area adjacent to the Sector no. 10, Uttara Model Town, Dhaka has been purchased. It is planned to construct a multistoried apartment for ORAA-85 members. Feasibility studies are going on for few other commercial projects. If any project is found feasible and viable, then it may be considered for implementation.
